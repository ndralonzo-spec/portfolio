A Journey Guided by Science, Skill, and Heart

General Dentist

My journey in dentistry has been shaped by a strong academic foundation and a genuine commitment to putting patients first. Studying biology and dental surgery at the University of the West Indies gave me the knowledge and clinical training to approach care with both precision and compassion.

I believe great dentistry is about more than treatment alone. It is about making patients feel informed, comfortable, and confident in their care. By combining restorative skill, surgical training, and a dedication to continued learning, I aim to provide thoughtful, high-quality care that supports lasting oral health for children, adults, and families.

Degree : Doctor of Dental Surgery, University of the West Indies
Foundation : B.Sc. in Biology, University of the West Indies
Internship : Full-time clinical internship at UWI St. Augustine
Learning : Continuing education in rotary endodontics and patient-centered clinical care
